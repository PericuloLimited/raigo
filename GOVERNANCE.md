# RAIGO Governance Model

This document outlines the governance model for the RAIGO project. Our goal is to ensure the project remains open, transparent, and responsive to the needs of the community while maintaining a high standard of quality and security.

## Project Roles

### 1. Users
Users are anyone who uses RAIGO in their projects. We value feedback from users, and encourage them to participate in discussions, report bugs, and request features.

### 2. Contributors
Contributors are community members who have contributed to the project in some way, such as by submitting code, documentation, or participating in discussions. All contributors must follow the [Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).

### 3. Maintainers
Maintainers are responsible for the day-to-day management of the project. They review pull requests, manage issues, and ensure the project adheres to its technical vision and standards.

Current Maintainers:
* Periculo Security Core Team

### 4. Steering Committee
The Steering Committee is responsible for the long-term vision and strategic direction of the project. They make decisions on major architectural changes, roadmap prioritization, and the appointment of new maintainers.

## Decision Making Process

We strive for consensus in all decisions. When consensus cannot be reached, the Steering Committee has the final say.

### Technical Decisions
*   **Minor Changes:** Bug fixes, documentation updates, and small features can be approved by a single maintainer.
*   **Major Changes:** Significant architectural changes, new targets, or modifications to the `.raigo` specification require a formal proposal (RFC) and approval from at least two maintainers.

### Governance Changes
Changes to this governance model require a formal proposal and approval from the Steering Committee.

## Adding New Maintainers

Contributors who have demonstrated a sustained commitment to the project and a deep understanding of its technical architecture may be nominated to become maintainers. Nominations are reviewed and approved by the Steering Committee.
